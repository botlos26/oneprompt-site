FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Is this prompt engineering?

No. Prompt engineering requires technical knowledge of how AI models work. OnePrompt is a guided translator — you answer simple questions in plain English, and it structures your thoughts into an effective prompt. No technical skills needed.

Does it work with ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, and Copilot?

Yes! OnePrompt creates universal prompts you can copy and paste into any AI tool. It doesn't matter which AI you prefer — the improved prompts work everywhere.

Does OnePrompt send my text anywhere?

Absolutely not. OnePrompt is completely local-first with no backend servers. Everything happens on your device. Your prompts never leave your phone.

Does OnePrompt call an AI model?

No. OnePrompt doesn't run AI or connect to any language models. It's a smart template system that helps you organize your thoughts into structured prompts. You then paste those prompts into your preferred AI tool.

How many templates are available?

OnePrompt ships with 14 ready-made templates covering email, writing, research, recipes, travel, fitness, social media, meetings, and more. Each template guides you through the right questions to get better AI results.
