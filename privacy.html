Privacy Policy

OnePrompt is built on a simple belief: your data belongs to you, not us.

The Short Version

  • ✓Everything you type stays on your device. We never see it.
  • ✓No account required. No sign-up, no email, no password.
  • ✓No analytics tracking your behavior inside the app.
  • ✓No data sold or shared with third parties. Ever.
  • ✓No ads. No ad networks. No tracking pixels.

What We Collect

Nothing. OnePrompt operates entirely on your device. Your prompts, your answers, your history — none of it is transmitted to our servers because we don't have servers that receive it.

All data you enter — your intent descriptions, question answers, generated prompts, and saved history — is stored locally on your iPhone using standard iOS storage. It stays there until you delete the app.

App Store & Analytics

OnePrompt is distributed through Apple's App Store. Apple may collect certain usage statistics as part of their platform (such as crash reports and download counts). This data is governed by Apple's Privacy Policy and is separate from anything we control.

We do not use any third-party analytics SDKs (no Firebase, no Mixpanel, no Amplitude, no Amplitude, no Facebook SDK).

Waitlist & Website

If you sign up for early access on our website (oneprompt.info), we collect:

  • Your email address
  • Your responses to the waitlist questions

This information is stored in Google Forms / Google Sheets and is used solely to notify you when the app is available and to understand user needs. We will never use it for marketing unrelated to OnePrompt.

You can request deletion of your waitlist data at any time by emailing us.

Children's Privacy

OnePrompt is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children. The app is rated 4+ on the App Store only because it contains no objectionable content — not because it is intended for young children.

Changes to This Policy

If we make changes to this privacy policy, we'll update this page with a new date. We'll only make changes that maintain or strengthen your privacy protections — never ones that reduce them.

If we ever introduce any data collection in a future version of the app, it will be clearly disclosed, optional, and require your explicit consent.

Your Rights

Since we don't collect personal data from the app, there's nothing for us to delete, export, or correct on our end. Your data lives on your device — you can delete it at any time by deleting the app.

For waitlist data, you have the right to access, correct, or delete your information. Just reach out.
